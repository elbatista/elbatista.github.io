About me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in the Distributed Systems Group at Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), Lugano, in a cotutelle de thèse program with PUC-RS, Brazil. My research focuses on dependable distributed systems, including State Machine Replication, parallel scheduling, and Atomic Multicast algorithms. I hold a M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Computer Science from PUC-RS and have published in top venues in distributed computing.

Before academia, I spent over a decade as a Software Developer and System Analyst, specializing in full-stack web development with JavaScript, React and Node.js. I also worked with Firebase, REST APIs, and CI/CD pipelines. I enjoy exploring how distributed systems principles can enhance web application performance and reliability.

I’m passionate about teaching and mentoring, with experience as a TA in distributed algorithms, operating systems, and systems programming. I’m always open to new research collaborations or discussions—feel free to reach out via email or connect on LinkedIn.

Research

I am currently a Ph.D. researcher in the Distributed Systems Group at Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), Lugano, Switzerland, under a cotutelle de thèse program with PUC-RS, Brazil. My research focuses on distributed dependable systems, including State Machine Replication (SMR), parallel scheduling, state management, and recovery. I am currently working on optimized data structures for SMR state transfer and Atomic Multicast algorithms. I hold a M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Computer Science from PUC-RS and I have published in leading journals and conferences in the Distributed Computing Community. Before academia, I worked for over a decade as a senior software developer and system analyst.
